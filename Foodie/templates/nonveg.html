Non-Vegetarian Delights in Lucknow

Explore the rich culinary heritage of Lucknow with our curated selection of non-vegetarian restaurants. From traditional Mughlai dishes to contemporary Awadhi cuisine, find the perfect dining experience to satisfy your cravings.

4.5 • Mughlai

Royal Spices

Experience the authentic taste of Mughlai cuisine in an ambiance fit for royalty. Indulge in succulent kebabs, biryanis, and rich curries prepared with traditional recipes and the finest ingredients.

4.2 • Awadhi

The Awadhi Table

Discover the culinary treasures of Awadh at The Awadhi Table. Savor signature dishes like Galouti Kebab and Nihari, known for their exquisite flavors and delicate textures. Enjoy a dining experience that reflects the rich cultural heritage of Lucknow.

4.7 • Fusion

Spice Fusion

Spice Fusion offers a unique blend of traditional Lucknowi flavors with a modern twist. Explore innovative dishes that combine classic recipes with contemporary techniques, creating a memorable dining experience.

4.3 • Kebabs

Kebab Corner

Kebab Corner is a haven for kebab lovers, offering a wide variety of grilled and roasted meats. From tender seekh kebabs to flavorful tikkas, each dish is prepared with precision and care, ensuring a delightful culinary journey.

4.6 • Biryani

Biryani Palace

Biryani Palace is renowned for its aromatic and flavorful biryanis, cooked to perfection with long-grain rice and tender meat. Choose from a range of biryani styles, each with its unique blend of spices and cooking techniques.
