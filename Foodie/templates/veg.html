Vegetarian Delights in Lucknow

Explore the finest vegetarian restaurants in Lucknow, offering a range of cuisines from traditional North Indian to contemporary South Indian.

The Royal Garden

Experience the rich flavors of North Indian cuisine in an ambiance that reflects Lucknow's royal heritage. Known for its exquisite paneer dishes and aromatic biryanis.

Spice Route

A culinary journey through South India, offering a variety of dosas, idlis, and uttapams. Enjoy a taste of authentic South Indian spices and flavors.

Green Leaf Bistro

A modern bistro serving a fusion of Indian and international vegetarian dishes. Perfect for those seeking a contemporary dining experience with a focus on fresh, organic ingredients.

The Saffron Table

Known for its elegant ambiance and a menu that highlights the best of Mughlai vegetarian cuisine. Indulge in their signature saffron-infused dishes and rich desserts.

Urban Greens

A casual dining spot offering a variety of vegetarian street food and quick bites. Ideal for a relaxed meal with friends or family, featuring a vibrant and lively atmosphere.
